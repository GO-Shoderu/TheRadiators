About Us

We are 4 University of Pretoria students studying BIS Multimedia. Over the course of our studies we have attained skills necessary to make us succeed in the workplace and to do so with ease.

We made different projects for our respective modules in order to showcase the skills that we have learned such as Programming in various programming languages such as C++, Java and JavaScript.

We are also skilled designers because we partook in a module called VIO which taught us almost all we know about design. The design principles we learned from this module are how to make use of colours effectively such that the colour combination does not hurt the user’s eyes.

We also learned how to maintain a consistent theme within our apps and websites regarding colours and the shapes of our elements.
